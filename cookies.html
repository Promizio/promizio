Cookie Policy

Last updated: 1 June 2026

Short version: Promizio uses only essential cookies required to keep you logged in and keep the platform working. We do not use advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit. Some cookies are essential for the website to function; others are used for analytics or advertising.

2. What cookies Promizio uses

We keep our cookie usage to an absolute minimum. We use only:

CookiePurposeTypeDuration
sb-auth-tokenKeeps you logged in to your Promizio account (set by Supabase)EssentialSession / 1 year
sb-refresh-tokenRefreshes your login session automatically (set by Supabase)Essential1 year

We do not use:

  • Analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics)
  • Advertising or retargeting cookies
  • Social media tracking cookies
  • Third-party profiling cookies of any kind

3. Essential cookies and consent

The cookies listed above are strictly necessary for the platform to function. Under GDPR and the Danish Cookie Order (cookiebekendtgørelsen), strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent. We do not display a cookie consent banner because we do not use any non-essential cookies.

If we ever introduce analytics or other non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and implement a proper consent mechanism before doing so.

4. Third-party cookies

Our authentication provider, Supabase, may set cookies as part of their service. These are used solely for authentication purposes. Supabase's privacy practices are available at supabase.com/privacy.

If you pay for a subscription, Stripe may set cookies during the checkout process. Stripe's cookie policy is available at stripe.com/cookies-policy.

5. How to control cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that deleting essential cookies will log you out of the platform and may prevent some features from working correctly.

Browser instructions for managing cookies:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

6. Changes to this policy

If we change our cookie practices, we will update this page. If we introduce non-essential cookies, we will implement consent tools before doing so.
